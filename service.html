Service & Engagement

Outreach

I constantly engage in outreach events. I give public talks frequently at events, schools and universities. I'm an invited speaker at Cambridge and Guildford astronomical societies, the Cambridge Science Festival and at Institute of Physics events.
I coordinate the Stars group outreach programme at the Institute of Astronomy by managing Window To The Stars, a colourful user-friendly pedagogical graphical interface to visualise the internal structure and evolution of a star, developed by Robert Izzard and Evert Glebbeek, during IoA open evenings. In Lebanon, I took part in the Science Days public events.

Community Service

I previously worked with MySchoolPulse, a non-profit NGO for schooling children with life-threatening illness. I also served on the Graduate Students Society at AUB. I'm on the LOC of several conferences and I currently serve on various committees.
